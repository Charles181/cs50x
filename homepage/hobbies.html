My hobbies

I've always considered myself a nerdy guy with some particular taste for hobbies. Here are some of them:

D&D

As most of you know, Dungeons and Dragons is a Table Top Role Playing Game, where all you need is pen, paper, some strange-shaped dice and your imagination to become whoever you want to be!

And you know what makes it even better for me? Getting to play D&D with my sons!

Programming

I don't consider myself a software developer, nor an experienced programmer, but I love learning new stuff all the time. I've been learning Python, Django, HTML, CSS, Javascript and a little of C. Now I know the basics and maybe a little more, but I'm always up to acquire more knowledge!

Videogames

Even though I don't have enough time like I did on my 20s, I still love spending some minutes playing on my computer, my nintendo Switch or my Xbox. Wether it's some Minecraft, Mario Party, any Zelda game, Mario Kart or some text-based RPG, I'm on!
